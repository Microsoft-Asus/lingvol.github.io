Unfortunately, health hasn't either been an enough reason for me to stop eating large amounts of junk food, sugar, soda and other foods of low nutritional value. Regardless of what might have resulted in my weight gain, I knew that it couldn't continue.

20th of July 2015 (92kg), I decided that I'd lose my body fat and increase (or at least maintain my muscle mass), until the point where I would be able to see my abs. Since I'd never in my entire life been fit enough to were I could see my own abs. With 18th of October as my end-date and 75kg as my goal, I knew there'd be lots of difficult challenges waiting for me down the road.

My determination combined with Hasse's knowledge and experience (one of my closest friends), resulted in me completely crushing my goal. Down the road lot's of people have criticized me, told me that it's not possible in such a short period of time, that I'm starving myself, that I'm training too hard, too long, too much... "You can't stop someone who knows where they are going".
