In the synoptic gospels, we read of the story of Jesus healing the woman who had an issue of blood. The woman had tried unsuccessfully for twelve long years to be healed. According to the Law of Moses, because she constantly was bleeding she was considered ritually unclean, and thus should not touch anyone else as they would become unclean as well. She also was unable to worship at the Temple as she was always in a state of ritual impurity. The healing touch of Jesus restored her to everyday life and temple worship, the same blessing He can do for us.
