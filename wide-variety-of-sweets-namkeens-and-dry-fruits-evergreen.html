EVERGREEN SWEET HOUSE, situated in a prominent market area in the South Delhi, India is a known name to millions of food lovers across Delhi and overseas, for over four decades. The idea nurtured by its promoter Late Shri Radha Krishan Chopra - himself a skilled Halwai (Chef) and very much deft in his trade, in early - 1960's to provide sweet - tooth customers a place where they can have the rich traditional Indian sweets in the best of taste and quality, is seen very much flourishing. The baton - command later passed on to his sons - Mr. Mohanlal Chopra, being assisted by Shri Akhtiari Lal Chopra and Shri Shyam Lal Chopra to fulfill his arduous task of service through taste and quality. The trio trained themselves from the grass-root level for better co-ordination, control, and rapport with the labour force. The work force at every individual planks and counts is made to feel home and responsible for the working and success of the company.
