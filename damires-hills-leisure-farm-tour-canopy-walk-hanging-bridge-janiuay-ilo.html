Damires Hills Tierra Verde Leisure Farm is located at Barangay Damires, Janiuay, Iloilo. It is a leisure farm that offers accommodation for overnight and day staycations. The main attraction of the resort is its farm view, the three swimming pools, the canopy walk, and the hanging bridge. The resort have also atv and zip line.
