This simple shoulder Micro Workout works every head of the deltoid as well as the upper back and traps for a solid. all-around shoulder workout.

Workout tips:
- Keep your glutes and hamstrings tight during the Y-flys to prevent sagging in your hips.
- Squeeze your shoulder blades together with body exercises.
- Keep a straight wrist during the Y-fly.
