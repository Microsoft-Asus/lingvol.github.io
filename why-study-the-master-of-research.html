The Master of Research (MRes) at Western Sydney University is a two-year postgraduate research training degree that provides you with a pathway to a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) and a career in research.

The Master of Research is open to applications from local and international students. For more information about the program and how to apply, visit: http://bit.ly/2ofFjC3
