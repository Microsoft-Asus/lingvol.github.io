ABOUT THE EVENT

Merging the overt world of Hollywood with the covert mission of CIA, this panel seeks to demystify the CIA's mission by comparing what's seen on TV to what happens in reality. Former CIA officers will join the acclaimed creator and actors from FX's The Americans on-stage for a lively discussion about the facts vs. fiction of espionage, CIA tradecraft, and living under cover.

Moderated by Joe Weisberg, former CIA officer and creator of FX's “The Americans”

Panelists:

Keri Russell, actress portraying Elizabeth Jennings on FX’s The Americans

Martha Peterson, former CIA officer and author of the book “The Widow Spy”

Mark Kelton, former Chief of CIA's Counterintelligence Center

Matthew Rhys, actor portraying Phillip Jennings on FX's The Americans

Costa Ronin, actor portraying Oleg Burov on FX's The Americans
