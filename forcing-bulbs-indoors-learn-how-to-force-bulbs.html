Forcing bulbs indoors is incredibly easy! You don't even need potting soil to grow bulbs indoors. By forcing bulbs, you'll have beautiful greenery for weeks with a show stopping finish!

Amaryllis blooms are known for their vibrant colors! Try mixing and matching different colors to create a beautiful bouquet!

My particular favorite is Paper White (Narcissus) bulbs. These bulbs are the dainty cousin of the Daffodil and are known for their delicate flowers and intoxicating scent.
