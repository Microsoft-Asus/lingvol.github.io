Zero the Hero helps kids exercise and learn to count to 100. This is a great song to engage your children in movement and practicing how to count to 100. Also, this song is perfect to have some Zero the Hero fun to celebrate 100 days of school.

This count to 100 song features the popular fictional character Zero the Hero who flies in with Zeroes so children can count beyond the number 9. Zero the Hero places the zero (0) to hold the place for the tens (10's), hundreds (100's), thousands (1,000's) and on to infinity. Wow, those zeroes are important! Zero the Hero is a big, cool, super character that helps make math fun. At your school or home, anyone can dress up a little bit and have fun with playing the part of Zero the Hero.

Teachers and parents all over the world do dress up as Zero the Hero to help children understand how important those zeroes (0's) are to hold the place for numbers to go beyond single digit numbers.

So, count to 100 and follow Zero the Hero while exercising, getting smarter, stronger and healthier.
