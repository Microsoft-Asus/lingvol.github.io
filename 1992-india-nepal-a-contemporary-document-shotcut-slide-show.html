What you see in this slide show are non professional but impressive photos taken on a trip cross country in India from the very north in Ladakh to the very south in Kerala. For us 23-year-old german students at that time it was an impressive experience. And it¬¥s still! We went to the Himalayas, we've seen Kathmandu a long time before the earthquake happened in 2015 that destroyed many historic buildings. Many people died, probably some of those we met, some might be on these pictures. Who knows? We also visited Kashmir, where there was a long term civil war. Impressiv for us was to see Varanasi, where Hindu burn their dead. One of the most fascinating things we ever did was a bus trip on Manali road, the so called Manali-Leh-Highway. And there was much more for us to see. We would like to invite you being part of the experience of travelling. Enjoy the soundtrack. This documentation is dedicated to the sister and the two brothers of Pushpa Mehtab. We met them in Indore. Pushpa went to school at the Nandanagar Social Welfare Center, where Mother Baptista, a dutch sister, took care of her education. Pushpa died the year after we met. She was lovely.
