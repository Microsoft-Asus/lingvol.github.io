"Shouldn't Have to Be Like That" is a single by Norwegian new wave group Fra Lippo Lippi, released from their third album 'Songs' in 1986. It was released as a double A-side with the song "The Distance Between Us".

In 2005, the song was remixed and released by Molella under the title "Lost Love" l.
There is also a cover version from the Japanese band Zabadak.
