〓Event Duration〓
2020/12/11 10:00 – 2020/12/18 03:59

〓Eligibility〓
Reach Adventure Rank 20 or above

〓Gameplay Overview〓
During the event, take on orders from the While It's Warm page of the event menu, then follow the map to navigate to Sara at Good Hunter, Smiley Yanxiao at Wanmin Restaurant, or Chef Mao at Wanmin Restaurant accordingly.

Each order comes with a few special requirements, such as:
● Deliver within the time limit
● No sprinting
● No gliding
● No climbing
● Do not take DMG
● Do not become affected by any element
Make sure to read the requirements carefully before starting your deliveries!

〓Event Rewards〓
● The event lasts 7 days with 3 new orders available each day. Each completed order brings a reward, and completing all 3 orders in a day brings a bonus reward.
● Orders refresh at 04:00 each day, and so do the rewards. Travelers should claim their rewards promptly after completing orders.

〓Notes〓
Each order can be completed only once. An order cannot be attempted twice, nor its reward claimed twice.
You can only accept one order at a time. Please complete the order you have currently accepted before taking another one. Travelers can check the current order under Story Quests in the Quest Menu~
Unfinished orders will not carry over to the next day, so please complete them in good time to avoid disappointment~
