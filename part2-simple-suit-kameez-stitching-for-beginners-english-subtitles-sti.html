In this video I will tell you how to stitch a simple suit properly and step by step.
This video is only about Stitching.
Cutting of the suit/kurti was done in the previous video.
This is especially for beginners who want to learn the cutting and stitching of simple suit/kurti/kurta.
For this you will need 2.5 mt fabric or according to your measurement. You will also need fusing/bukram for making neckline.
Topic I have covered in this video are:-
✓ How to properly join the neckline and making proper corners in the neck design.
✓ How to join shoulders (तीरा) with proper finishing and basic information related to that.
✓ Detailed information about joining the front and back part of the kurti.
✓ How to make chaak (चाक) properly.
✓ How join half sleeves.
And all the small and important information on simple suit Cutting and Stitching.

This is especially for beginners.
