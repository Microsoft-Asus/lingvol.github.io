Samdech Techo Hun Sen Inspects Road Construction in Sihanoukville.

  Samdech Akka Moha Sena Padei Techo Hun Sen, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, on the morning of June 2, 2020, inspected the construction of the road in Sihanoukville, a strategic plan for making Sihanoukville a tourist city and Civilized trade.

  It should be reminded that Samdech Techo Hun Sen has decided to release a budget of approximately US $ 300 million to build 34 roads in Sihanoukville, the first efficient road in Cambodia. The 34 lanes were opened by the Samdech Techo in November 16, 2019. After the groundwork, the construction of the road was stormed day and night with the utmost diligence by the company and the engineering forces of Prime Minister Hun Sen.

  In order to ensure the quality of the construction and to complete the plan by the end of 2020, HE Chea Sophara, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Land Management, Urban Planning and Construction and directly supervised the construction, as well as provincial authorities and officials concerned. Pay close attention to the construction process.

  According to the construction report, as of May 16, 2020, six months after the start of construction, construction was more than 90 percent faster than the projected 4.5 times. Percent.

  Please note that the 34 lanes have a total length of over 82 km in Sihanoukville. The 34 lanes are divided into three types:

  1: Three boulevards spanning over 22 km.
  2: There are 16 roads with almost 50 kilometers in length.
  3: 15 subways with a length of almost 12 km.
  With the aim of transforming Sihanoukville into a modern, environmentally friendly city for living, tourism and business.

  In particular, ensuring high quality, durability and durability, the construction of the 34 lanes must adhere to the following principles and technical specifications:
  1: Comply with the Land Management Plan of Preah Sihanouk Province 2030 Vision
  2: Determine the axis of the road along the center of the public road feed along the cadastral map.
  3) Construction of detailed technical plan of each road studied by the National Committee for Coastal Management and Development in accordance with the Ministry of Public Works and Transport's standards.
  In addition, the main roads will also be equipped with security cameras.

  It should be highlighted that the Royal Government of Cambodia has set out a plan to develop the province of Preah Sihanouk with the aim of 2030 to develop and develop the province of Sihanoukville as a national development in the coastal area of ​​Cambodia through the development of the modern city, the most beautiful beach resort. A global, multi-purpose, multi-purpose, multi-purpose, international gateway, a national growth center, a national logistics center in association with Asean and global markets, and a high-tech manufacturing industry that is harmonized with a better living environment. The construction of these 34 luxury roads is an important contribution to the achievement of the Royal Government's 2030 vision for the development of Sihanoukville.
