In today's video, Dr. McNair shares his views on life, the importance of financial literacy & history, and Youngstown's very own Buckeye Review.

Dr. McNair is a Marriage Specialist, Relationship Coach, Love Doctor, and Counselor. He designs customized couple solutions and provides group seminars and workshops on the dynamics of relationships and thinking skills. He has been called upon to lecture and counsel on the purpose of marriage and the power of sound relationships.

As a Pastoral Counselor, Licensed Clinical Social Worker and pastor, he has co-facilitated Yellow Ribbon Military Family Workshops (Chicago, Orlando, Virginia Beach, Nemacolin Woodlands, PA, Kalahari, & Pittsburgh) and is often the featured speaker at marriage and family conferences. Served as Recovery Coach for Veterans, is a retired Air Force Captain and co-Director, with his wife, Dr. Linda McNair, of NAME (National Assn. of Marriage Enhancement) Center Youngstown, a community resource for free nouthetic (biblical) marriage counseling, coaching, and training for CMS (Certified Marriage Specialist) Couples.

He has specialized training in Gottman Couples Therapy ( Level 1, 2 & 3)) and training in IBCT (Integrated Behavior Couple Therapy). Research interests in African American marriages, relationships, and spiritual integration in evidence-based counseling. He is also a columnist and Publisher of the African American newspaper, The Buckeye Review.
