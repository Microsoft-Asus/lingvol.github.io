Is a 5 Minute Chart in Forex a Good Timeframe to Trade? For me I like 5-minute charts. Now if you're swing trading, 5-minute charts are probably too low for you; you'll need 15 minute, hourly, daily charts and above. The good thing about 5 minute charts is that you can generally fit a couple of days worth of trades into your window which to me is the key aspect for picking a timeframe.
