The Greater Austin Business Awards, now in its 20th year, celebrates businesses in the region, recognizing the people and companies that have demonstrated a unique vision, innovation, and best business practices.

We recognize COVID-19 has affected businesses across Austin in a variety of ways, resulting in new innovations and adjustments to business models, as they find new ways to make a difference. Join us as we recognize diverse industry leaders and companies that are innovating, managing through adversity, and impacting the community.
