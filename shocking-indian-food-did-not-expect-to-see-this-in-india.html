ADDRESS: Gat No. 477, Old Mumbai-Pune Road, Kamshet, Talmaval, Naigaon, Maharashtra 410405, India
OPEN: 10AM - 1AM

Toni Daa Dhaba opened for business in 1984 and has been serving delicious meals to the people of Mumbai ever since.

ü•òKADAKNATH CHICKEN GRAVY: Boil black chicken pieces in water, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric and salt. For the gravy: Add oil in a pan, fry ginger, green chili, chopped onion, chopped tomato, ginger-garlic paste, green chili paste, chicken masala, salt and in-house black gravy (caramelized onion and ginger-garlic, green chili and coriander cooked till brown). Once fried, add boiled black chicken pieces, coriander leaves and some stock then allow it to cook until ready.

ü•öEMU EGG BHURJI: Blow the egg shell from one side to get the egg yolk and white out. Whisk the giant egg. In a pan, add oil and fry chopped onions, green chili paste, chopped tomatoes, salt, turmeric, chopped coriander and the whisked egg. 1 emu egg = 6 chicken eggs

üçòROOMALI KHAKRA: Mix banana, refined flour and egg together, then roast it on an upside down tawa pan to make roomali roti-style thin bread. Garnish with butter and peanut masala.
