Message: The Loyalty of God
Scripture: Romans 11:1-5

God foreknew you. Whatever you're going through, have gone through, and will go through, comes as no shock to Him. He isn't put off by a checkered past. God's loyalty? It knows no bounds. Perfection isn't required to get or keep Him. He'll sit there with you all the while; when you're hurting, in your foolishness, and after you rebel — He's not getting up from His seat that's right by your side. Have you been looking for solid ground to stand on? It's the character of our loyal, loving God!
