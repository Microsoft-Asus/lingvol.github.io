DELICIOUS FOOD IDEAS AND RECIPES FOR BEGINNERS CHEFS



Let’s get back into the kitchen and create some magical food you’ll want to try today. I show you how to come up with your own creative recipes without any effort just by using simple ingredients from your cupboard.

I give you my delicious corn-dog recipes with pickles. Simply, take a hole in the jerkins and place a hot dog through. Then dip your corndog in batter, fry it in some oil and Bon Appetite.

I also show you a delicious egg recipe. Prepare some hard-boiled eggs and then remove the shells, dip them in some flour, egg wash, and then coconut flakes. After that, fry your eggs in oil and enjoy.

I also show you how to make apple donuts. Take a few apple slices, dip them in batter and then fry them in oil. Serve them with cinnamon and chocolate sauce.
