The House of Commons Petitions Committee has scheduled a debate in Westminster Hall on the motion “That this House has considered e-petitions 326066, 550846, 316404 and 549015 relating to the impact of Covid-19 on schools and exams”.

Jonathan Gullis MP, member of the Petitions Committee, will open the debate. The Department for Education will send a Minister to respond.

What are petitions debates?

Petitions debates are ‘general’ debates which allow MPs from all parties to discuss the important issues raised by one or more petitions, and put their concerns to Government Ministers.
