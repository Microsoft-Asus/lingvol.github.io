[ never know ]
1. You never know. - (그건) 모르는거야.
(You can never know / You can never know for sure)
2. You never know when you might need them again.
(그것들이 언제 또 필요할지는 모르는거야)
3. You never know what someone is really thinking.
(사람의 진짜 속마음은 모르는거야)
4. You can never know for sure what someone is really thinking.
(사람의 진짜 속마음을 알 수는 없어)

[ know better ]
5. Come on, you know better than to believe everything you see on the Internet.
(에이 왜그래, 인터넷에서 보이는걸 다 믿으면 안된다는거 잘 알잖아)
6. I thought you knew better by now than to argue with Jacob. He's (like) the most stubborn person I know.
(지금쯤이면 제이콥이랑 말씨름하면 안된다는걸 알 줄 알았는데. 걔는 내가 아는 사람 중에 가장 고집이 센 사람이야.)
7. I should've known better. (그러지 말아야 한다는걸 알았어야 했는데)

[ not know the first thing about ]
8. When I was just starting out as an English teacher, I didn't know the first thing about grammar.
(내가 영어 강사로 처음을 시작했을 땐 문법의 '문'자로 몰랐어)
