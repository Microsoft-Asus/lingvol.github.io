The Long Run is a gem of a track birthed out of the collaborative fusion of the groovy vocals and guitar of ELIH along with the delicate yet driving production of BLOOM. The track carries a special summertime vibe with an uplifting energy which offers the listener an opportunity to lighten up and connect with the sun inside. The groovy guitar complimented by a hypnotic synth and the soulful vocals of ELIH fuse to open hearts and inspire ecstatic movement.
