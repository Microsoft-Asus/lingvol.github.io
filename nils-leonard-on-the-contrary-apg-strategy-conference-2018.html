APG Strategy Conference | On the Contrary
8 October 2018
Speaker: Nils Leonard, Founder at Uncommon London

Nils talked about how actually it can sometime help to get angry about something – provided you then put your energy into doing something about it. Because if we get angry, we get personal about an issue and the more personal it is, the more effort we put in to solving it. He left us with the thought; What kind of company would you create if you wanted to create one that people in the real world wished existed?
