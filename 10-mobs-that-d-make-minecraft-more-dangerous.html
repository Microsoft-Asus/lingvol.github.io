Today we’re looking at 10 new mobs that will make Minecraft more challenging across all dimensions. You could now encounter Mosquitos in swamps, allowing you to get the Amber Mosquito to resurrect the Dodo bird. Or you could find Lightning Bugs in the Nether, who are friendly until attacked, but give you the ability to make Lightning Arrows with their drop. These arrows will come in handy when fighting every dimension’s Envirolord. The Aquatic Envirolord doesn’t like boats, and will throw you and your boat far away. The Infernal Envirolord will use fire and fireballs to attack, but it’s drop, the Infernal Sceptre, will give you that power too. The Zephyric Envirolord will push you away in a tornado, but its drop will make your elytra flying a ton easier. Which new dangerous mob is your favorite? Let us know in the comments below!
