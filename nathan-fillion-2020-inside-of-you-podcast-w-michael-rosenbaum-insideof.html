Nathan Fillion (Firefly, Castle) joins me this week for an episode largely centered around the idea of managing expectations throughout life.

Nathan shares how he’s had to manage expectations of new projects after personal experience with disappointing ones that he thought would be hits and others that were surprise cult classics.

He also talks about managing expectations of his relationships with his parents, helping tame the expectations of actors to get the best out of them on set, and curbing the expectations on things you cannot control versus the things that are within your power.

Awesome episode folks, hope you enjoy it.
