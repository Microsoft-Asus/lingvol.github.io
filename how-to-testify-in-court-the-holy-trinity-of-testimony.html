Testifying should be simple. Too many get it wrong too often. Undoing a bad answer is like putting toothpaste back in the tube. Try as you might, it ain't happening. The key is understanding your role and surrendering to the process.

Visit my Blog to learn all my DOs and DON'Ts of testifying (http://craigpenney.com/Blog-Toronto-Criminal-Lawyer-Do-Dont-Testifying.html). Read the reactions of Judges from Court transcripts.

"How" you testify is just as important as what you have to say. After you testify, it will be too late. The time to get ready is now.
