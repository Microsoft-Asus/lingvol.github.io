In this video author and mental health educator Douglas Bloch shares a simple but powerful coping strategy for healing from depression. It is to plan activities that you can look forward to, even if it is something as simple as taking a walk with a friend or eating your favorite ice cream. The anticipation of some good to come stimulates the pleasure center in the brain and gives one a reason to get up in the morning..
