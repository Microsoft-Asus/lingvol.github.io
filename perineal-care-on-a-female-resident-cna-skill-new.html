View how this skill should look when performing for the Florida CNA State Exam. View to proper way to clean a female's perineal area, as well as viewing how to properly clean and store your supplies. Cleaning strokes must be made from the top down. This skill requires comprehension of infection control procedures to reduce the possibility of bacterial infection resulting in a urinary tract infection for the patient. This skill is one of the 21 testable skills on the Florida State CNA exam and is also used in RN and LPN training.
