From newsgathering to the production and distribution of news and information, AI is behind more and more tools used by newsrooms worldwide.

What are the most recent AI developments for journalism and how are newsrooms adapting to the new technologies?

In this session, we hear from top newsroom managers about the latest trends and what we should expect to see in 2021.
