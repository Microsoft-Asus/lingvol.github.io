Made from stainless steel and full-grain leather, the materials are excellent as are all of Trayvax’s wallets. With an adjustable leather component to accommodate more cards and a great cash clip/strap, cards and cash are secure. The only oddity is the protrusion of the bottle opener and lanyard eyelet. As a potential to scratch up other items in the same pocket and poke holes in pants, it’s an odd design element…but you get a bottle opener out of it!
