Many people are still in the active throes of Borderline Personality. They have not come to the awareness of the need for professional help to give them a chance to to learn about accountability and personal responsibility. To make this shift, for people with BPD, means dealing with a profound lack of emotional intelligence. This process requires, first new awareness, secondly learning to take accountability and personal responsibility which is a process of emotional maturation.
