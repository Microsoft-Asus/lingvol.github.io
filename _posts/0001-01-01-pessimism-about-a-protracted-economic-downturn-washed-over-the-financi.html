[Notes:ANCHOR VOICE]

DOREEN MOGAVERO "I think it's fairly clear that we're close if not already in a recession"

IT WASN'T THE SEVEN HUNDRED BILLION DOLLAR BAIL OUT PLAN THAT DROVE MARKETS THURSDAY....IT WAS UNSETTLING ECONOMIC DATA

DOREEN MOGAVERO "I think this bail out plan has been such a story over these past couple weeks that I dont think we've had chance to focus in on the economic data that's been coming out and it's hasn't been very good

DAVID WYSS "we've already had 8 consecutive declines in payroll employment right now we're looking at 100,000 job loss"
