From the ballpark to the lunch box to the Halloween candy bowl, peanuts are a popular part of the modern diet. But peanut can cause a mild to a severe allergic reaction.

Common symptoms of a mild allergic reaction to peanuts can include stomach ache, runny nose, itchy eyes, hives, or tingling of lips and tongue. Some people may have a more severe reaction called anaphylaxis.
