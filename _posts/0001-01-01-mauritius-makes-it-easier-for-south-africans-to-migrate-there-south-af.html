--- title: "Mauritius makes it easier for South Africans to migrate there | South Africa" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/MKVKMLSc7MM\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "MKVKMLSc7MM" categories: "People-Blogs" tags: ["mauritius","south africa"] date: "2020-12-07T13:23:05+03:00" vid_date: "2020-06-21T21:59:48Z" duration: "PT7M12S" viewcount: "12017" likeCount: "599" dislikeCount: "16" channel: "Loving Life" --- It will soon be easier and cheaper for South Africans to emigrate to Mauritius from this month, says property group Chas Everitt International.

As part of its coronavirus budget on 4 June, the government announced a number of incentives to encourage foreigners to live, work and invest in Mauritius.

In line with new government measures introduced, the minimum investment required to acquire an occupation permit (OP) as an investor, and live in Mauritius as a non-citizen, has been halved to $50,000 (R867,000), from $100,000 (R1.7 million).
