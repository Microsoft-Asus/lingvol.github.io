--- title: "너무나 헷갈리는 no more than, not more than 깔끔하게 정리" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/TtE7eIhcpsk\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "TtE7eIhcpsk" categories: "Education" tags: ["화상영어","전화영어","영어표현"] date: "2020-12-03T08:00:31+03:00" vid_date: "2020-10-19T15:00:07Z" duration: "PT3M32S" viewcount: "202" likeCount: "5" dislikeCount: "0" channel: "플루언톡대표" --- 1. no more than : 단지 ~에 지나지 않는

ex) My allowance is no more than 10,000 won per a week.
[내 용돈은 일주일에 고작 1만원이야.]

ex) My office is no more than a km apart from my house.
[내 사무실은 우리 집에서 겨우 1km 떨어져 있어.]




2. not more than : 많아야, 기껏해야

ex) Everybody was furious to hear that he was sentenced to not more than 12 years in prison.
[그가 기껏해야 12년 형을 받았는데 모두가 격노했다.]

ex) I have not more than 2 to 3 customers a day because of the COVID-19 pandemic.
[코로나 때문에 하루에 손님이 2-3명 밖에 없어요.]
