BETTER focuses on those chronic diseases showing strong evidence that prevention and screening improves outcomes. Using a collaborative approach, BETTER provides a framework for a paradigm shift from fragmented disease-specific management to a proactive, personalised, comprehensive approach that engages patients as active participants in their own health.
