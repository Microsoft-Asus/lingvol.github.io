In this video, I print a range of models with the Creality LD-002R. This was a pre-production model and based off the comments in the LD-002R Facebook group, some of the issues that annoyed me in this review have been fixed already.

I didn't have a failed print while testing this unit, and the results are quite good. This seems to be on par with most of the other printers in this price range. Please inspect my results closely as well as those from other reviews and make a decision from there.
