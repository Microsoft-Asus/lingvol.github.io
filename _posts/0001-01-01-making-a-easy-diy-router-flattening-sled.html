In this video we are going to make a very easy and quick router flattening sled that can be used for live edge slabs as well as large glued up panels that will not fit through the planer. So I have been asked to make a walnut live edge coffee table but I did not have a flattening sled and i figured I would make it a separate video so the actual build video will not be as long. The sled can be made in a afternoon and for little money.

I hope this video helps if you have been want to make one of these but were not sure how to.
