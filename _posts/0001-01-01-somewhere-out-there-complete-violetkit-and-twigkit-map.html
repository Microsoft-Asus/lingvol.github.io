ITS DONE AND ITS PERFECT! This MAP turned out better than I could have hoped with so many wonderful animators taking part.

Im honored to have had some many talented people express their interest in this MAP and I even got to discover new animators I hadnt seen before! I wish you could have all join, I really do.

This MAP is based on Twigkit and Violetkit from "A Vision of Shadows", in the early stages of their relationship, just after being seperated and put into differant clans. The sisters are two of my favorite warriors characters and are both endearing and heartbreaking to read about.

I hope you enjoy this MAP project as much as I do.
