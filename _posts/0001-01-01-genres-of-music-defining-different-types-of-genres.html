Throughout history, artists from different genres tend to be identified with a single genre. However, they can make music across many different types.

Understanding different types of musical genre help you write music and is a huge part of your musical evolution. Even if you don’t like some genres, it’s still important to understand what makes them different to help you find your own sound.
