In March 2019, we got to be a part of the film crew shooting the first ever Adventure Racing World Series event in the country - Expedition India 2019. It was a 450KM non-stop race from Manali to Shimla, with over 20 teams participating from all around the world. The course was divided into different legs - hiking, cycling, rafting - to cover the entire distance.

We got to work with an amazing film crew from South Africa - Elevated Adventure Films - and had a blast shooting with them! :)
