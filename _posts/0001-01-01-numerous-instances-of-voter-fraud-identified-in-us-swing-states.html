“Everything from Trump ballots being found discarded in a river to irregularities, signature verification failures,” Ms Devine told Sky News host Alan Jones.

“Mail-in ballot are known to be vulnerable to fraud and this was an election with a record number of ballots.”

Ms Devine said the democratic machines in cities such as Atlanta, Detroit and Philadelphia used the pandemic to change the rules around voting and then they performed “industrial scale ballot harvesting”.

“So, whether it was illegal or legal, it was still absolutely unfair,” she said.
