POOMWON is a company specializing in importing and processing the lamb from Australia, with the aim of popularizing lamb. The company has launched its own brand, “Lamb Masked Wolf,” and it has provided diversified cuts of lamb. Considering the increasing number of people who enjoy camping, the processed products have gained popularity. We introduce POOMWON, which has gained trust from consumers.
