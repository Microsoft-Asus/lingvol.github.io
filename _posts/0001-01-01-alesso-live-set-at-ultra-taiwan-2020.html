Last night I got to play a festival for the first time in 8 months. It felt as I expected: INCREDIBLE!
In order to do this show my team and I had to quarantine for 14 days. It’s something we had never done before but we made it thru. Taiwan hasn’t had a single local Covid case in 200 days, Without a vaccine! It’s inspiring and gives me hope for the rest of the world that are dealing with this unfortunate virus. Thank you to my whole team and everyone that made last night happen! Not sure when we will be able to all be together again like this but I know I won’t be forgetting this moment anytime soon. Lots of love /A
