Mirror Principles:

1. The world is a giant dual mirror and reflects your relationship to it.
2. The reflection is formed in the unity of heart and mind
3. There is a delay in the reaction of the dual mirror.
4. The mirror states the content of the relationship and ignores the nature of the relationship.
5. Move your attention from the reflection to the image
