Play it cool with this versatile Polish slang word: “spoko.”

Although you’ll probably use it most often to say “ok,” it has many other meanings. It can also mean “alright,” “sure,” “no problem,” or “don’t worry.”

You’ll also hear locals using it to mean “calm down.” This makes sense because it comes from the word “spokojnie” which means “calmly”.

In this video, you’ll learn many ways to use the word “spoko.”
