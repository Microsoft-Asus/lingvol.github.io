Learners in lower grades are already obliged to take history.
The task team led by Professor Sibusiso Ndlovu was established in 2015. It was asked to conduct a comparative global study on how best to implement the introduction of history as a compulsory subject in grades 10, 11 and 12. The team says that history should be enhanced, as it offers problem-solving and analytical skills.
Its report has recommended that history be phased in from 2023 with the process leading to the phasing out of Life Orientation.
