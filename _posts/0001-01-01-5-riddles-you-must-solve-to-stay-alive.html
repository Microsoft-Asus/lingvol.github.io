SUMMARY
-You’ll have to choose one of 3 options as to what to do when you find yourself face-to-face with a ferocious lion.
-Try to pick the best option for when you’ve been bitten by a venomous snake.
-What on Earth can you do to protect yourself out in the African grasslands against this rhinoceros?
-Which of the doors in an empty scary castle would you choose given that one of them leads to "Death by drowning", the second one threatens you with "Death by electric chair", and the third informs you that you’ll meet "Death by machine guns"? There is also a forth one but it says “Remain in the castle and starve to death”.
-Choose one of the 4 elevators you see in front of you to escape.
-Pick one of 4 doors knowing that there are sinister things waiting for you behind those doors.
