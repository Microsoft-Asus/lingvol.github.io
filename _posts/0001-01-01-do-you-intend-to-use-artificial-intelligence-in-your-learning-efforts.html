On September 13-14, 2017, Sarder TV & Future Workplace hosted the CLO Insight Summit at Microsoft Technology Center-NYC.

The CLO Insight Summit is a hands-on executive development program that brings to life concepts from practitioners who are redefining corporate learning, human resources, and talent management. This conference focus on how Chief Learning Officers are preparing for the future workforce and workplace to deliver learning at the speed of business.
