--- title: "Why the Teensy is the Best Arduino (although it's not)" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/75IvTqRwNsE\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "75IvTqRwNsE" categories: "Science-Technology" tags: ["arduino","teensy 3.2","teensy 3.6"] date: "2020-12-03T17:32:17+03:00" vid_date: "2020-11-28T17:00:05Z" duration: "PT11M13S" viewcount: "43699" likeCount: "4240" dislikeCount: "66" channel: "Zack Freedman" --- I put a Teensy in almost every electronics project, and here's why: it beats the Arduino Uno, Mega, even Portenta in memory, size, and sheer power, but it's just as easy to use.

The Teensy is cheap, and with the new Teensy 4.0, it's extremely powerful. Its generous flash and RAM, numerous peripherals, and smallest size make it perfect for my most creative ideas. I've used them in machine learning wearables, Among Us gadgets, mechanical keyboards, PC case mods, and more. Even my Raspberry Pi projects get a Teensy!
