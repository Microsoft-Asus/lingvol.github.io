It involves assessing and redesigning working environments in order to reduce the likelihood of harm occurring to workers, clients, subcontractors, or any person coming through a working area. The foundation documentation of health and safety in NSW is derived is the Workplace Health & Safety Act 2011 (NSW). The Systems we develop must legally abide by recommended WHS Principals.
