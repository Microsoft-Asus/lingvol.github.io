Thank you for all the amazing comments. Many of you asked where I got the inspirations from. The lyrics were inspired by a fairytale story by Hans Christian Andersen called 'Under The Willow Tree'. I always wanted to write a song that's 3/4 time waltz so this is what I got :) Really happy that you enjoyed it!
