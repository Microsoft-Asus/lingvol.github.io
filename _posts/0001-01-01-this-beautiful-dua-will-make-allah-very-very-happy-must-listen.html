This Dua Will make Allah happy !

اللّٰهُمَّ اجْعَلْ غَفْلَۃَ النَّاسِ لَنَا ذِکْرًا، وَاجْعَلْ ذِکْرَہُمْ لَنَا شُکْرًا، الحمد لله و الشكر لله ، سبحان الله، الله أكبرلا حول ولا قوة إلا بالله
O Allah, the people have carelessly neglected Your remembrance, let us remember you and when they turn to your remembrance, let us thank you. Praise and Thanks to Allah, Glory to Allah, Allah is the Greatest, There is no might nor power except in Allah.
Ameen My ALLAH SWT :’)
