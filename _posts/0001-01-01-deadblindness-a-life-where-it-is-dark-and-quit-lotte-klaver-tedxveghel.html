When she started her study as photographer, she learned that she suffered from Usher Syndrome, an incurable genetic disease that causes deafness and blindness. She was 17 at that moment.
Also her youngest sister Roos, at that moment 15, got the same message.

At this moment she travels, lives life at the fullest and uses her voice to create awareness for a disease that has been known and identified for over 100 years but not cure has been found (yet).
