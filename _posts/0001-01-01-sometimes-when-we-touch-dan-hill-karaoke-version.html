"Sometimes When We Touch" is a 1977 ballad written by Dan Hill (lyrics) and Barry Mann (music) on the album Longer Fuse, but was also released as a single in 1978. It was Hill's biggest hit, peaking at # 3 on the United States Billboard Hot 100 and #10 on the Adult Contemporary chart
