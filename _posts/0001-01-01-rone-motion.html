After completing a 12 minute composition, Rone worked with orchestral arranger Romain Allender to record the piece with the orchestra Les Siècles, accompanied by pianist Vanessa Wagner, and conducted by François-Xavier Roth. The track will be released to raise money for the non-profit organisation Orchestres à l’école which aims to support the development and advancement of orchestras in schools. You may hear some nuances from the last ad Société Générale in “Motion”…
