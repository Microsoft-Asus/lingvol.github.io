The official YouTube channel of Atlantic Records artist Max Frost. Subscribe for the latest music videos, performances, and more.

Named by both NPR and Rolling Stone as one of “10 Artists You Need To Know,” Max Frost has earned acclaim with his unique musical merging of soul, funk, hip-hop, and pop.

Max has previously released two EP’s, “Low High Low” (2013) and “Intoxication” (2015), featuring four consecutive singles to hit #1 on HypeMachine’s “Most Popular Tracks on Blogs Now”. His 2018 single "'Good Morning" also made waves in PepsiCo's bubly Sparkling Water campaign, as well as the ESPN show Get Up!

Max has previously toured with Gary Clark Jr., Fitz & The Tantrums, HONNE and Wild Child. In the fall of 2018, Max will be headlining The Gold Rush Tour with Mikey Mike and UPSAHL as supporting acts.
