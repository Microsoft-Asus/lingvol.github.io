Moojibaba begins this Satsang by asking us if we have found the happiness that doesn’t change, the peace which is so vast it leaves room even for negative emotions to have their play.

To find this peace, we have to turn our perspective around—we know the objects of perception but what about the subject?

“Is it worth our attention to look at that? Are you an object in front of the lens of perception, or behind the lens of perception?

Don’t give me an answer—you must be the answer.”
