--- title: "Health Ministry says it will need more time to produce free facemasks" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/rPq9uZ2eE2Q\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "rPq9uZ2eE2Q" categories: "News-Politics" tags: ["Uganda","Kampala","NTV Uganda"] date: "2020-12-03T06:28:09+03:00" vid_date: "2020-05-29T18:45:49Z" duration: "PT2M46S" viewcount: "323" likeCount: "2" dislikeCount: "3" channel: "NTVUganda" --- The permanent secretary in the ministry of health has said government will not be able to procure and distribute the over 30 million facemasks to be given to each Ugandan above the age of 6 in fulfillment of one of the prerequisites for further easing of restrictions put in place to curb the spread of Covid-19.
Dr. Diana Atwiine says it will take at least one month for government to produce and distribute the facemasks due to delays in procuring suitable manufacturers.
During his 14th National Address on government's COVID-19 response, President Museveni said services like public transport would only resume after every Ugandan had been given a face mask.
