Aya Matsuura, Chief Technical Advisor of Way out of Informality project, talks of the varied challenges for workers in the informal economy who despite contributing to local economies often go unrecognized and excluded from labour laws. On top of this, they also don’t enjoy the ‘worker’ status and tend to face discriminatory practices and poor employment conditions.
