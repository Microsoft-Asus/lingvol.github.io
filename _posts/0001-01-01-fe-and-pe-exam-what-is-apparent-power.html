In the FE exam or PE exam, you may be given voltage and current in phasor format or magnitude and angle and would be asked to calculate the apparent or complex power. This video provides an example with a given line-to-line voltage and line current and asks to calculate the complex power. It is important to know how to use given quantities properly to calculate complex or apparent power. You should not mix line to line and per phase quantities.
