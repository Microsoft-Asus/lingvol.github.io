Going to school is never really anyone’s favorite thing to do. But it is one of those necessary things we all need to go through to become well-rounded people. School often involves long days in the classroom, dry lectures, and teachers who are sometimes just there for a paycheck. We’re not saying that this happens everywhere, but those who’ve had not-so-great experiences in school know exactly what we’re talking about. But don’t be discouraged! We are in a golden age of education right now. Technology is being used all over the classrooms these days, and new teaching methods are inciting passion instead of boredom. We’re seeing a trend of schools breaking away from the traditional format to create something new. The schools that we’ll be talking about have their students running to class in total excitement for a new day of learning. We just wish that we were lucky enough to have that same classroom experience.
