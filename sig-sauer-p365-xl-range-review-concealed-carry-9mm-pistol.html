Welcome back to Hunt Fish Shoot. This week we bring you a concealed carry special, the Sig Sauer P365 XL. The Sig 365 XL is an excellent choice for a self defense firearm which can be easily concealed with close to full size pistol capacity. A great gun for any CCW carrier! Check it out!

The Sig P365XL has a 3.7 inch barrel, X-ray night sights, extended beaver tail, one 12 round and 15 round magazine. The improvements you get over the standard P365 are well
worth it especially with the flat faced trigger. The trigger is the finer point of this pistol with a 90 degree break and crisp reset.

If you are looking of a concealed carry pistol give the P365XL a hard look and you will be more than happy with it. A straight up competitor to the Glock 48 and it doesn’t disappoint!
