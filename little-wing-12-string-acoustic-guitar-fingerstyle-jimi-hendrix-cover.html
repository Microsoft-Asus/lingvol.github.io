I’ve been really diggin’ this new “Blue Gc-CM 12” 12-String from Furch Guitars. It’s very easy to play for a 12-string and the tone is punchy, well-balanced, and full. I also like that it has a cutaway, which is great for playing this kind of 12-string lead, but also great for when you want to double parts with a high capo to fatten up strumming arrangements in the studio.
For this arrangement of Little Wing, I tuned the 12-string down a whole step, which gives the guitar a slinky, bluesier tone. Some of my favorite 12-string performances were by both Jimi Hendrix and Stevie Ray Vaughan. Blind Willie McTell and Leadbelly are a couple of my other favorite 12-string players.
