The lips are arguably one of the most expressive parts of the human face. Psychologist Albert Mehrabian once described the 7-38-55 rule. That 7% of our communication is verbal and 93% is non-verbal. Expressive elements such as the lips and eyes are important to aesthetic because how they look at rest determines how you’re perceived. Fuller, functional lips appear youthful and desirable while protruding, unfunctional lips indicate underlying dental conditions.
