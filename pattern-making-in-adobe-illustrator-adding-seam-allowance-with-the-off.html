Learn how to add instant seam allowance to your digital sewing patterns/ basic blocks/ Slopers with our quick video tutorial. It's an update on an outdated technique. To apply seam allowance to your digital pattern, simply select the pattern outline, navigate to "Object/ Path/ Offset Path" Add the amount of seam allowance you need in the first field and then click done. Instant seam allowance without having to create a brush!

We also explain how to add seam allowance to darts. This is sometimes called adding a witch's hat.
