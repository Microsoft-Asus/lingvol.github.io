America works overtime to create a colorblind society, but does this colorblindness perpetuate, rather than resolve, racism? Despite a growing racial divide, attorney, activist and author Traci Ellis says the time is now to have the courageous conversation about the damage done in the name of colorblindness. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree from Florida A & M University and a Juris Doctor degree at The Ohio State University.

In addition to her professional pursuits, Traci is also very active in her community.
