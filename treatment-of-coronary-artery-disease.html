Join us for the final lecture on Coronary Artery Disease. During this lecture Ninja Nerd Medicine will teach you everything that you need to know about the Treatment of Coronary Artery Disease. Zach will discuss the emergent treatment protocol for CAD which consists of MONA-- Morphine, Oxygen, Nitrates, Aspirin. Next, we discuss the types of interventions that are performed if a patient is experiencing a STEMI. This will involve the gold standard treatment of coronary angioplasty with stent placement. The alternative treatment is also discussed which is thrombolytics, primarily consisting of tPA. Finally, the lecture is concluded with the types of medications that are commonly used for the long term management of Coronary Artery Disease.
