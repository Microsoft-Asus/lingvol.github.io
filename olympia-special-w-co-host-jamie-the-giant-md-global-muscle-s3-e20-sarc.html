It's the Season 3 Finale & Olympia preview and prediction special episode guys!

For this extra-EXTRA-special episode, host Giles 'Tiger' Thomas has a GIANT co-host in the form of the sensational new pro 6'5" 330lb colossus that is real-life Superman Jamie Christian-Johal.

Jamie sent shockwaves through the Pro League in 2020 with his pro debut in Alicante and London, but Jamie's magnetic personality in this in-studio appearance proves why he is so much more than just a human Jumbo Jet.

For our 2020 Olympia rundown, the guys over in the UK draft in three experts to find out how they feel the show is going to play itself out.

First they have last year's 6 place Mr Olympia finisher; the Texan Giant Steve Kuclo, then we have pro legend Dennis James (gives us the inside scoop on Big Ramy as well) and finally; the Beautiful Mind that is all-era's historian Milos Sarcev.
