For this extra-EXTRA-special episode, host Giles 'Tiger' Thomas has a GIANT co-host in the form of the sensational new pro 6'5" 330lb colossus that is real-life Superman Jamie Christian-Johal.

Jamie sent shockwaves through the Pro League in 2020 with his pro debut in Alicante and London, but Jamie's magnetic personality in this in-studio appearance proves why he is so much more than just a human Jumbo Jet.
