USA Network and Universal Cable Productions’ beloved legal drama SUITS celebrated its upcoming 100th episode milestone today at ATX Television Festival with a live table read of the pilot at the State Theater in Austin, Texas. In attendance was the full cast: Gabriel Macht, Patrick J. Adams, Rick Hoffman, Gina Torres, Sarah Rafferty and Meghan Markle, alongside executive producer and creator Aaron Korsh and moderator Robyn Ross (Entertainment Weekly). Nick Wechsler (“Revenge”) and Abigail Spencer (“Timeless,” SUITS recurring) joined to read additional roles.
