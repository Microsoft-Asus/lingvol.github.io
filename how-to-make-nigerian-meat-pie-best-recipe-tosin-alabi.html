Ingredients
recipe makes 22 pies
PASTRY:
6 Cups of plain flour
500g Butter
4 Tbs of Sugar
1 Tbs of baking powder
1 Tbs of salt
4 Eggs
Milk
FILLING:
750g minced meat
1 large potato
2 carrot sticks
sunflower oil
half bulb onion
1 scotch bonnet
seasoning (salt, pepper, knorr cubes)
2Tbs of flour to thicken stock.

GLAZE:
1 Egg

UTENSILS:
Rolling pin
Baking tray
Saucer
Mixing bowl
knife
fork
Table spoon
Bakers brush.

Bake at 350f or 180c
