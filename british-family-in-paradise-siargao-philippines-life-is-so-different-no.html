We are a British Family now living In PARADISE! SIARGAO Philippines is a stark contrast to our previous life. Everything is SO different now and we've seen this island go from tourist hotspot to personal oasis with nobody on the streets.

It has been a roller coaster year in 2020 with our whole situation being turned on it's head, but we couldn't have wished for a more perfect place for it to happen. It'll be very interesting to see how our 2021 will be.

Our names are Jmayel, Sacha & Story. We traveled to Siargao island with Jmayel's mum & Story's grandma just before the Global pandemic that sent the whole planet into quarantine. We got stranded for months and fell in love with island life, now we want to make that life more permanent.

Follow us on this journey :-)
