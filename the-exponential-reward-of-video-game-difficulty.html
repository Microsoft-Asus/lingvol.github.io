After all this time, I'd finally like to put my own hat into the difficulty discussion in gaming as a whole. We'll talk about difficulty in the "hardest games", how difficulty manifests in "easy games," and the difficulty levels that try to bridge the gap between. Through this, I hope to convey why I feel difficulty in video games is important.
