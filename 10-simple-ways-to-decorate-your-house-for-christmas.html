ADORABLE CHRISTMAS DECOR IDEAS

There are so many amazing ways to decorate your house for Christmas. We prepared for you the coolest ideas that you can DIY. The most exciting thing about our Christmas crafts collection is that you don't need to spend a lot of money on these crafts.

Do you know that you can make a cool Christmas tree using plastic spoons and forks. This is a cool way to upcycle plastic utencils. Decorate these trees with beads and gemstones. Use spray paint to color these trees. You can use green paint and go classic, or you can paint your tree blue or golden!
However, a white tree also looks very stylish.

You can make a bigger DIY Christmas tree using plastic bottles. Use green or transparent plastic and save a lot of money on buying a new Christmas tree from a shop.

There are so many cool ways to create unique Christmas ornaments! Leave a handprint on a Christmas ball and write your name on it. Choose smaller balls for little kids.

You can make adorable ornaments using yarn and glue – check out our amazing tutorial on making yarn ornaments. We made a beautiful star – what's your choice?

You cna make lovely ornaments using ribbons and sticks. Use a short stick as a trunk and colorful ribbons as Christmas tree branches.

Watch this video up to the end and see how to make amazing paper ornaments.
