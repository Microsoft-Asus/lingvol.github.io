John Farnham performs Help at the Whispering Jack concert.

Recorded live at the Melbourne Sports and Entertainment Centre, Melbourne in March 1987 and was broadcast on national TV. An edited VHS version (100 min) was released later in 1987. An edited DVD version (68 min) was included on the 20th Anniversary re-release of Whispering Jack on 25 November 2006.

Help was released in 1980 on the Uncovered album. It reached #8 on the Kent Music Report.
