The Vaccines are an English indie rock band, formed in West London in 2010 by Justin Hayward-Young (lead vocals, guitar), Freddie Cowan (lead guitar, vocals), Árni Árnason (bass, vocals) and Pete Robertson (drums, vocals). The band have released two studio albums, What Did You Expect from The Vaccines? (2011) and Come of Age (2012). This song appears on their debut album.
