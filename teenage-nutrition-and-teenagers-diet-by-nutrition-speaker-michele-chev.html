http://www.ahealthyview.com Excerpts from Teenage Nutrition and Teenagers Diet, presented by Nutrition Speaker Michele Chevalley Hedge of AHealthyView.com.au, to Queenwood Girls High School.

Included in this Nutrition for Teenagers presentation is vital information on;

• what are hidden sugars and hidden sugar effects
• what you can learn from reading a food label
• how our food effects your hair, skin, hormones and body shape
• how food effects your academic performance

Accomplished speaker Michele Hedge is a nutritionist and published author and a dynamic health advocate. Nutrition for teenagers is an extremely important topic for Michele, particularly as she has three teenagers herself.

As a nutrition speaker, Michele is in demand as schools realize the importance of teenage nutrition and how a teenager's diet affects their lives daily. And her nutrition presentation has a positive affect on the teenagers.

Testimonial:
"It's quite rare when you attend a lecture that really stays with you. This is the case following the great presentation you gave to the Queenwood students and parents. I use the word 'lecture' loosely since you never seemed to be lecturing the girls; rather you managed to present what can often be a rather dry, scientific topic with lightness, humour and logic -- all at level which the students could relate to. I think everyone was amazed how quickly 70 mins passed!" Richard Tier -- Sydney NSW
