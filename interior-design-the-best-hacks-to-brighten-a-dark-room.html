||More About Sharrah||

Sharrah Stevens has always believed that a beautiful home can change your life. With a passion for wanting to help others love where they live, Sharrah created Live Your Style as a source of inspiration for home lovers. Sharrah creates tip based videos on how to decorate, organize and clean your home to show others that they too can create a home their proud of! In 2019, Sharrah came together with her mother, interior designer, and fellow YouTuber, Rebecca Robeson from Robeson Design to create a new brand called Kinwoven.
