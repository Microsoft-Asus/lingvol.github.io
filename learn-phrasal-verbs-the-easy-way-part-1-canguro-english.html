Phrasal verbs in English are every student's worst nightmare. They are frustrating, confusing and seem to be completely random. But are they?

In part one of this series I will show you how cognitive linguistics can explain the hidden logic behind phrasal verbs and make them much easier to learn. Join me as I teach you the deeper meanings of the particles up, down, on, off, in and out. See you in class!
