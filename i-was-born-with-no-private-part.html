Hi, my name is Cate, and I was born with no private parts. I hope I could say it was a figure of speech, but it’s very real.
When I was a young kid, I didn’t realize anything was wrong. To me, it seemed completely normal! I thought every other child looked the way I did, and no one told me there was something different about me.
That was until we had a biology class about the reproductive system. The teacher explained all about the different organs and the distribution inside and outside the body. Suddenly I had so many questions, but I couldn’t ask them in front of all my classmates!
I rushed home that day and begged my mom for answers. I still remember how worried she looked. She went pale and sighed. She had been dreading this moment for a long time, and now she finally had to explain my condition.
It was the most confusing day of my entire life! Suddenly everything changed. I couldn’t believe what I was hearing, and in fact, ended up calling my mom a liar.
She told me I was born with no private parts, and that it was a rare condition. I could still go to the toilet normally, but I’d never be able to have children of my own. I completely freaked out, screamed at her, and rushed up to my room.
I didn’t want to speak to anyone after that and even ended up missing a few days of school. My parents understood I was struggling, so they didn’t force me to leave the house.
They did speak to me often and tried their best to make me understand what was going on and how it would affect my life.
Eventually, I went back to school, and everything seemed to go back to normal. I didn’t really understand how this would affect my life in the future. I was too young to comprehend it fully yet, so I remained my old happy self.
