With severe covid restrictions going on for most of the world, I've been very surprised how relaxed many of the Balkan countries have been and are readily welcoming tourism. I'm not saying that everyone SHOULD be traveling right now, but if you wondering what your options would include, the Balkans are most likely one of your best options (at least in December 2020). I've definitely adapted how I travel this year to stay as safe as possible, but I do believe that it is possible to travel AND stay safe for people who have low risk factors and aren't coming into close contact with many other people. Many of you guys had asked what it's like to go inbetween borders at this weird time, so I thought I'd film my land border crossing from North Macedonia to Albania. Hope you guys enjoy! xx
