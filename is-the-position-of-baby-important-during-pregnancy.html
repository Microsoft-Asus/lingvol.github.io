While it's important to pay attention to your posture throughout your life, it's even more important during pregnancy. During pregnancy, you see, your body's posture is naturally changing, with the curve of the lower back becoming more pronounced as you carry a heavy load in the abdominal area. Correct posture throughout pregnancy can help alleviate the lower back pain that is so common in pregnant women, and it can help get the baby in an ideal position for delivery.
