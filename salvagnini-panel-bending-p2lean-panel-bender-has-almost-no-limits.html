Salvagnini is revolutionizing traditional manufacturing concepts, extending the panel bender’s typical range of application and pushing the boundaries of traditional bending potential.
The Salvagnini P2lean panel bender is essentially a sheet metal processing centre capable of producing a wide range of items, making it a great tool for anyone, even businesses who have never contemplated the panel bender as their ideal type of bending machine.
The P2lean is also perfectly in line with market expectations when it comes to energy consumption: in this is a direct consequence of the machine’s new electric actuator, and the new finite element analysis of the structure.
