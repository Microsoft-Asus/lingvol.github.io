BODYBUILDER by day and drag queen by night - 28-year-old Rock, aka Miss Toto, dominates both worlds with sheer determination and a lot of sequins. Currently residing in Chicago, Illinois, Rock took up bodybuilding whilst still living in Miami. Rock who identifies as non-binary explains that Miami was the place where they first fell in love with bodybuilding and competing in the sport. Besides that, they soon found that drag and bodybuilding actually have a lot in common. Working out five days a week and having taken part in numerous bodybuilding competitions, the popular drag queen’s physique has made an impressive transformation. Having come across negative comments saying muscles and dressing in drag can’t go together, Rock is determined to prove people otherwise...
