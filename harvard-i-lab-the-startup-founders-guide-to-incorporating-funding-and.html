For startup founders, legal documents can be the cart that drives the horse, inundating them with esoteric language and expensive services that obscure critical corporate milestones. This workshop shows how to master the salient issues and delegate the boiler plate.

In this workshop, join Endeca Co-Founders Steve Papa and Pete Bell as they simulate the founding and early growth of a company, walking through key terms and legal due diligence.

You'll learn how to:
-Manage the legal process for key founding milestones around incorporation, funding, and governance
-Allocate shares to founders, investors, and employees
-Set terms around control, vesting, discounts, and participation
