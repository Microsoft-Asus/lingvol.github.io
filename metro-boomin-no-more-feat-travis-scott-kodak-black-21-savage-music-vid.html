Featuring 21 Savage, Kodak Black & Travis Scott
Produced by Metro Boomin
Album NOT ALL HEROES WEAR CAPES

“No More” is a dark-trap banger featuring 21 Savage, Kodak Black & Travis laced with intoxicating production by Metro Boomin.

The song finds the aforementioned rappers in an intoxicated “zombie”-esque state as they continue to “pop pills” and drink lean until they “can’t feel no more”.

Kodak Black and Travis Scott have previously collaborated on ZEZE, DJ Khaled “Down For Life” and on the “Roll in Peace” remix.

Kodak Black and 21 Savage both appeared on the XXL Freshmen Class of 2016, thus performing in the cypher for that year together.

Travis Scott and 21 Savage have collaborated most out of any vocal duo on the track on cuts like Outside, Ghostface Killers and NC-17.

Additionally, Metro Boomin has worked with all three vocalists before this song. Most notably on Tunnel Vision with Kodak (his highest streamed song), 3500 with Travis (the first single for his debut album Rodeo) and the Savage Mode and Without Warning tapes as well as Bank Account (Savage’s most streamed solo track).
