Well, we did it again. We were really lucky with the time for the contest,
as we had a planned Studio visit in the first 2 weeks of August, for our debut cd. Thus we decided to enter in the 2nd Song contest, after we were one of the winners last time. We knew we had to top our last entry, which resulted in a lot of pressure. But after some songwriting sessions we agreed to theme the song around the Pirates of the League, ahead of all,
Gangplank, the Captain. The last week was quite stressful, all of us were either running around in Pirate costumes, recording their Instruments, cutting the Video or asleep.
But it is done now. And we are happy with the result. Enjoy!
