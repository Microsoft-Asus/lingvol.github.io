A brief video going through a surrogate's expenses in the UK, including the average cost of surrogacy and what the expenses are used for.

There is a huge difference between altruistic and commercial surrogacy - it is not lucrative to be a surrogate in the UK, but equally it will not leave you out of pocket. Pregnancy can be a pricey journey.

A rough idea for Intended Parents in Britain how much money they will need to factor in for their surrogate's expenses.
