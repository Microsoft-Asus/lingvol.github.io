1) Losing Money - this is the first stage and its natural because 'logic' human psychology doesn't help with making money in the stock markets.
2) Losing less money.
3) Breakeven
4) Small Gains
5) Consistent gains
6) Expand on your best setups
7) Add extra trade setups
8) Increase size on best setups - notice how increasing trade size is at the very end.

The important take on this is to keep persisting and plodding on - the biggest stages will be 1) and 2). Most people drop-out here.
