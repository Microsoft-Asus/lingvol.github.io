The Enhanced (upgraded) Wolven Witcher Gear Set can be found in Velen & Kaer Morhen (Scavenger Hunt: Wolf School Gear Upgrade Diagrams - Part 1 & Part 2). It consists of 6 pieces - 4 armor pieces and 2 swords. You can buy the maps for this scavenger hunt from the master blacksmith in Novigrad & the blacksmith in Lindenvale. You must craft the upgrades at an Armorer and Blacksmith after collecting the upgrade diagrams. You must also have the Regular Wolven Set to upgrade to the Enhanced version. This is a medium armor witcher set.
There are 4 tiers to each armor set: Normal, Enhanced, Superior, Mastercrafted.

Recommended Character Level: 21
