A journey to Peru has become synonymous with a trip to the ancient Incan ruins of Machu Picchu, but many travelers don't realize that it is not the only significant Inca ruins found in the region.

On our recent visit to Cusco, we decided to take a path less traveled and embark on a hike with Apus Peru (http://www.apus-peru.com/) that would take us to Choquequirao, a lesser know, but an equally impressive set of ancient Inca ruins hidden deep in the Apurimac Valley.
