If you're a healthcare professional, knowing medical Spanish can help you communicate better with more patients. It also makes you more appealing to employers!

In this video, we’ll share some basic terms for nurses and other healthcare providers.

By the end of the video, you’ll know key phrases for exams, injections, medication, and more! For example, here are a few things you’ll learn how to say in Spanish:

-I need to examine you.
-I need to weigh you.
-I need to inject you.
-You need to take this medication.
-Please allow me to…
-Do you want to make an appointment?

In addition to these medical phrases, you’ll also learn some helpful vocabulary for things like pills, and measurements of liquid medication.
