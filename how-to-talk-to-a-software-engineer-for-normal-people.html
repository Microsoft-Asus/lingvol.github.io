Ex-Google TechLead explains how to talk to a programmer. This highly educational video should help normal, everyday people better understand & navigate social scenarios when in the presence of a software engineer or developer. Unbeknownst to you, a coder is often trained to think & act in a manner you might find unusual or even frustrating.
