Ex-Google TechLead explains how to talk to a programmer. This highly educational video should help normal, everyday people better understand & navigate social scenarios when in the presence of a software engineer or developer. In our current digital age, it is more and more likely that you will run into a programmer in your daily life, whether it be for professional or personal reasons. Unbeknownst to you, a coder is often trained to think & act in a manner you might find unusual or even frustrating. I hope this video can help clarify the reasons why, and how to deal with such situations if you ever find yourself in a dangerous scenario.
