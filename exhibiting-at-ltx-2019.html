I'm back and here's what it was like to exhibit my vintage computers at LTX 2019!

Booth tour with Gamers Nexus: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=87ALbovM5q0

This is a completely new type of video for me to make so you'll have to forgive any oddities with it. There are many aspects of editing this that were firsts for me, music segments, cropped to camera parts, vlogging, and filming away from my set in general.
