LESSON SUMMARY:

English listening is difficult for 3 reasons:
#1 - Many English words are pronounced differently from their written form
#2 - When native speakers talk fast, we "link" the words together.
#3 - Most English learners get too little English listening practice

English listening tips:
#1 - Practice every day - make it a habit!
#2 - Practice the RIGHT way - listen multiple times, and don't try to understand every word right away
