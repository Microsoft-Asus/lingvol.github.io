Garbage 20 Years Queer Tour encore of Push It (from album Version 2.0), Cherry Lips (Go Baby Go) and #1 Crush from 2001 album Beautifulgarbage. Alternative rock band was live in concert at the Greek Theater, Los Angeles, CA on October 8, 2015. Garbage members are Shirley Manson – vocals; Steve Marker – guitar, keyboards; Duke Erikson – bass guitar; Butch Vig – drums. Garbage performed Push It and Empty on Jimmy Kimmel Live Hollywood stage June 2016.
