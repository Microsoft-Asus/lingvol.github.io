Quick Fix Steps :
*Exit Teams if open
*Reset Internet explorer
* Rename Update.exe to Update.exe.old
C:\Users\yourname\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Teams
*Open Teams.exe from C:\Users\yourname\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Teams\current or previous

Disable automatic start, change ms team shortcut to
C:\Users\Yourname\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Teams\current\Teams.exe
from
C:\Users\Yourname\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Teams\Update.exe --processStart "Teams.exe"
