Let's go grocery shopping! In this video, we will learn vocabulary and phrases that you can use at a grocery store or supermarket.

Phrases used in the video:
1. Finding an item - Where can I find ___?
2. Asking for stock - Do you have more ___ in stock?
3. Asking if an item is sold - Do you sell ___?
4. Asking for the price - How much is it?
5. Talking about discount - Can you tell me where I can find ___ on sale?
6. Going to the checkout - I'll pay with ___.
7. Getting a refund - I would like to return ___.
