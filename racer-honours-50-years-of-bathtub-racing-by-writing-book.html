Nanaimo's Kevin Saunders, has been immersed in bathtub racing culture since he was a young boy. An avid racer himself, to honour 50 years of the World Championship Race, Kevin has written a book that looks back a half century to how the race started and the people who helped it become what it is today.

The 2016 Great International World Championship Bathtub Race in Nanaimo BC takes place on July 24th at Maffeo Sutton Park. www.bathtubbing.com.
