This talk was given at a local TEDx event, produced independently of the TED Conferences. Andrei Gómez Suárez explores the importance of dialogue, resorting to his experience in context of the peace talks in Colombia with the FARC. He describes the process of how the concept of dialogue brought about change in a country that suffered under violent conflicts for 50 years.

Andrei Gómez Suárez is a researcher for the National Centre for Historical Memory of the Department of Social Prosperity of the Colombian Government and associate researcher in International Relations at Sussex University, with a current focus on the Santos-FARC peace talks.
