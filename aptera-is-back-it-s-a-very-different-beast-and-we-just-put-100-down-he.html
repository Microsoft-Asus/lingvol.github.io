The Aptera three-wheeled electric car first broke cover more than a decade ago. Then it suffered the same fate as many startups at the time, with the firm declaring bankruptcy in 2011.

But now it's back - and on Friday last week it announced specifications and pricing for the newer, more efficient, more powerful reincarnation of the car that looks like it's from the future.

We look at the company's history, examine what's changed... and explain why Nikki just put herself on the waitlist to own one.
