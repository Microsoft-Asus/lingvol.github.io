Hate the gym? Don’t want to diet? There’s a mind technique that can help you shed pounds. More info: https://yurielkaim.com/visualization-for-weight-loss/

It has to do with the power of visualization. The body you want begins in your mind. Before you write this off as mumbo-jumbo, know there’s science behind it.

Researchers at the Cleveland Clinic had one group of people work out with weights, and had another group not work out at all – but they did mentally rehearse the same workout in their minds.

The visualizers increased their strength 13.5 percent without exercising at all.

When you take time to “see” what you want, your body will begin to follow along. Want to shed pounds? Picture it happening.

Your brain is powerful. Put it to work for you by visualizing what you want!
