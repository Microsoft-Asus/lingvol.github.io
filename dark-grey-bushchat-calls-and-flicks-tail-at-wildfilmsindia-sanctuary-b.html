The smartly attired black eye striped Dark Gray Bush Chat sits on a tiny twig and trills, while flicking up its tail with each trill. What a cute little Himalayan bird this is, breeding at the Wilderness Orchard botanical gardens and wildlife sanctuary at Jabberkhet in the western Himalaya. It balances delicately, adjusting for each breezy wisp that threatens to throw it off its perch!
