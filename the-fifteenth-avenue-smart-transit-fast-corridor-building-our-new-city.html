Liverpool City Council has unveiled its futuristic “first contact” vision of how the future Western Sydney International Airport will be connected to Sydney.

The FAST Corridor will be a key gateway for visitors to Australia showcasing the unique natural environment of South West Sydney. The corridor will be inviting and vegetated with transport infrastructure naturally forming part of the landscape
