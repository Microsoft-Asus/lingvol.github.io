a visiopreneur is someone that sees the picture of a better tomorrow and then works hard to bring it to reality. A dreamer and a doer all in one.

I am a visiopreneur. And Iam also an engineer and a businessman that build up a business over the last 15 years. one year back i had a liver transplant and my body, that was functional before the transplant but sick, started to heal completely.

This made me think what will happen if a number of people forgot about themselves and become the new liver to be transplanted in a sick and broken world to bring healing to the world.
