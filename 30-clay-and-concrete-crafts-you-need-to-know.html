AMAZING THINGS YOU CAN MAKE WITH CEMENT



To help you get creative and give some color to your stuff around you, I demonstrate some tips and tutorials using both clay, concrete, and a bunch of other tools.

For starters, take an ice-cream cone, fill it with cement, and then use it to organize your jewelry.

Another great DIY hack is to add cement in a Ziplock bag, then pour it in an ice tray, add some paper clips on the top, and then use them as little paper organizers for your desk.

You can also make amazing furniture using cement. Or, if you have old books, recycle them into a stand for your lamp by soaking them into cement.

You can also use an old candy container as a mold for your cement. After it dries, apply some gold acrylic paint to it to give it some color and then attach a ring to it to make beautiful jewelry.
