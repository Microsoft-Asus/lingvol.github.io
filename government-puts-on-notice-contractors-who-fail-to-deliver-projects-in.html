All contractors superintending over stalled state-sponsored projects have been put on notice. While disclosing that the government has been forced to reassign some of the projects already in process due to non-completion, Interior cabinet secretary Dr. Fred Matiang’i warned that the state will arrest and prosecute contractors who fail to deliver projects on time. He made the proclamation after complains from western Kenya leaders who decried decades of shoddy infrastructural works in the region.
