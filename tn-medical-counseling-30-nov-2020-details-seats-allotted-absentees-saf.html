Tamil Nadu has started the general counseling for MBBS/ BDS admission for the year 2020 on 23 Nov 2020. Unfortunately, due to Nivar Cyclone, no counseling could happen from 24 Nov to 29 Nov 2020. So, Day 2 counseling continued on 30 Nov 2020 with the allocation of seats from Open Category seats.
In this video, I have discussed the seats allotted on 30 Nov 2020, number of absentees and total seats allotted so far. I have also covered the closing rank for colleges where seats have closed for certain categories.
This video also forecasts the predicted safe score for various categories to get into Category A, that is government medical colleges.
