Super typhoon Goni brought intense rains and caused heavy flooding killing at least 10 people and causing volcanic mudflows to bury houses. Video shows a river with strong currents overflowing its broken concrete embankment in the southern part of the Philippines' main island of Luzon. Goni is the world's strongest storm so far this year with winds reaching up to 225kph (140 miles per hour) and gusts of up to 310kph (190mph) as it continues to cross the main island
