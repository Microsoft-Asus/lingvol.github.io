This video is about whether ridicule has a place in rational discourse. It isn't a simple yes or no answer, but instead depends on the context of the ridicule. A lot of people commonly will use the existence of ridicule in an argument as a way to disregard the argument as a whole, but as this video will explain that is itself a disruption to public discourse.
