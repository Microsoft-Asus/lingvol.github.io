Inexpensive Compact Z119 Extremely Popular and Comfortable Home
Price: Est costs 94,129 PLN ($26,590)
Size: Usable area 117 + 30 m²

Z119 is an extremely popular, comfortable home project. Greatly integrates into any, even small plot. Compact, straight and compact dimensions make it an easy and inexpensive investment. The bright colors of the walls perfectly match the wooden facade and the gray tiles.
The ground floor consists of a spacious living room with a fireplace and an exit to the covered terrace. The kitchen is partly separated from the living room by the ingenious layout of the pantry. There is also a single room garage with a large utility room . Upstairs there are three comfortable bedrooms. There is also a spacious wardrobe and a shared bathroom. The master bedroom has its own separate bathroom with a window and a dressing room.
Z119 is a charming, exceptionally attractive project. The small size and graceful, solid shape are just some of its many advantages.
