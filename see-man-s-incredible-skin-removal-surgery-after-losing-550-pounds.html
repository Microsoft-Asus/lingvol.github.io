Like so many Americans, Fred struggled with obesity. At 750 pounds, he felt like a prisoner in his own body. With diet and exercise alone, Fred lost 550 pounds making him the biggest weight loss guest in The Doctors history. His transformation left him with extreme excess skin. Watch as plastic surgeons Dr. Kamran Azad and Dr. Roger Bassin help Fred get rid of 17 pounds of loose skin. Fred shares that the skin removal has increased his mobility and he’s started cycling and motorcycle riding and volunteering with animal organizations.
