Linus Gillgren (college and pro golfer) and Fabian Spiess (professional soccer player) talk about Professional and college sports, and the importance of educating and preparing yourself about the effects professional and college sports will have on your life. They also talk about the importance and responsibility an athletes family has on their journey to become a successful athlete.

Linus Gillgren and Fabian Spiess both work for Athletes USA - A University search company that helps Individuals that want to become a student Athlete in the United States and UK. https://athletesusa.org
