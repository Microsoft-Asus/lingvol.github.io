In the move from BS4 to BS6, the Skoda Rapid has ditched its old 1.6 MPI petrol and 1.5 TDI diesel engines in favour of a 110hp 1.0 TSI three-cylinder turbo-petrol engine. It also gets a new 6-speed manual gearbox and a torque converter automatic will follow later. There are a few more updates too, but is that enough to breathe new life into this ageing midsize sedan. Hormazd Sorabjee drives it to find out just that.
