This is the second excerpt from the 3rd session of our Ethics in an Age of Informality webcast series, titled The Ethically Attuned Therapist. This excerpt features Mary Jo Barrett, M.S.W., founder and director of the Center for Contextual Change, teacher at the University of Chicago, and coauthor of "Systematic Treatment of Incest." In this session, Barrett provides practical guidance on how to establish boundaries from the first session.

Ethics in an Age of Informality is a 6-session, streaming-video webcast series that can be purchased with or without 6 CEs. The series features interview-style sessions with leading therapists in the field including Lisa Ferentz, Mitchell Handelsman, Mary Jo Barrett, Frederic Reamer, DeeAnna Nagel, and Kenneth Hardy with moderator Rich Simon.
