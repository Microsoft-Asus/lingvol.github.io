Meaning of obdurate : stubbornly refusing to change one’s opinion
Examples
1. The President remains obdurate on the question of tax cuts.
2. He remained obdurate, refusing to alter his decision.
3. Sometimes, you are too obdurate for your own good!
4. Because Janice was obdurate, she did not shed a single tear while hearing the tragic story.
5. Despite the defendant’s apology, the judge was obdurate and gave him a thirty-year sentence.
6. The protestors were obdurate and did not move even when the police arrived.
7. Will you stop being so obdurate and at least listen to my point of view?
8. Obdurate and quick to judge, the detective believed every suspect was guilty.
9. They argued, but he remained obdurate.
10. She is of obdurate temperament, and gets angry easily.
