This video is about the follwing given English expressions which are used to
express any kind of 'Habit' of either present, past or future.

* get used to
* is/are/am + used to
* used to
.............................................................................

Get used to
...........
Ex: Mujhe chay peene ki adat ho gayi hai.
Use khana pakane ki adat ho rahi hai.
Rule[Sub + h.v. + get used to + v+in/ob]

Ex: I have got used to taking tea.
He has got used to cooking food.
I didn't get used to that environment.
................................................................................
is/are/am + used to
....................
Ex: Mujhe chay peene ki adat hai.
Mujhe subah jaldi uthne ki adat hai.
Rule[Sub + is/are/am + used to + v+ing/ob]

Ex: I am used to taking tea.
I am used to waking up early in the morning.
She is not used to going to temple everyday.
....................................................................................

Used to
........
Ex: jab main tenth class me that mai cirkcket khela karta tha.
two saal pahle main nacha karta tha.
Rule[Sub + used to + v1 + ob]

Ex: When I was in class 10th, I used to play cricket.
Two years ago, I used to dance.
Two years ago, I didn't use to dance.
Did you use to dance, two years ago?
