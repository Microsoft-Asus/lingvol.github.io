In this video we look at the very under used strategy of betting on draws in the football/soccer market. Predicting draws can be a difficult process but with the aid of this video you will be able to pick matches that have a good chance of ending all square. By doing so you will be able to make profitable selection in the 1x2 betting market allowing you to build a healthy profit.
By using an increasing stakes method we can protect ourselves against long winless (or drawless) sequences by ensuring that when we do hit that draw our profits will be reinstated.
Please always practice good bankroll management and only bet what you are comfortable with losing.
