SURPRISING USES FOR COMMON HOUSEHOLD ITEMS

Here is a collection of ideas on how to transform ordinary things into something special. Storage boxes may cost a lot but we will show you how to make cute storage boxes from ordinary cardboard boxes. You will need an ordinary box and a rope. Use hot glue gun to secure one and of the rope and wrap the box with it. Cut your old t-shirt and place in the box. Ready! Did you know that flat iron could be used to curl hair? Moreover, use a flat iron to iron ribbons and collars. Find out how to fix an unexpected hole in your favorite shirt. Just use no sewing trick – use a hot flat iron and cover the hole with a small embroidered patch that is for sale in many fabric stores.
Here is a collection of unexpected uses of such an ordinary thing as panty liners: if you have forgotten slippers turn panty liners into emergency slippers; forget about embarrassing sweat stains and stick a panty liner to the sports jacket; if you need insoles to get warm when it’s cold outside, use sanitary pads; use a panty liner to wash out a makeup, pour micellar water on it; make a night mask out of a sanitary pad; use panty liners to hide camel toe when you wear leggings; pour water on sanitary pad and freeze to make an ice pack.
Wrap the remote control with wrapping plastic prevent food stains while watching a favorite TV show. Make a cool door stopper out of an empty paint tube. The design of this project is so realistic that everybody will think that some paint has been squeezed out and leaked all over the floor. Use perfume to renew your dry marker and share the result with friends. Make a cool USB Flash driver out of empty lip balm container.
