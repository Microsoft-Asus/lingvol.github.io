If your tent gets stuffy in the warmer months, then the Lithium Ion Fans from Coleman are a summer camping must-have.

With three models to choose from – the 6”, 8” and 12”, there will be a size in this range that will suit your tent or caravan set up. They come with a rechargeable lithium ion battery, LED lights that can be operated independently and you can run them on 12V or 240V as it comes with adapters included.
