How do we increase awareness and understanding of illnesses that are at least visibly, invisible? In this talk Bethany Dawson, who has for the last six years lived alongside the mental, physical, and social effects of having a chronic invisible illnes, shares her insights and explores how we can bring about change Bethany Dawson is a first-year Politics and Sociology student who has for the last six years, lived alongside the mental, physical, and social effects of having a chronic invisible illness. She will be taking the TED stage to raise awareness and share her experiences of living with an invisible illness.
