Developing integrated weed management strategies while ensuring herbicides don’t lose their efficacy is one of the biggest challenges facing the Australian grains sector.

GRDC research compiled by the CSIRO has found WA growers incur yield losses of about $25/ha due to weeds and spend up to $85/ha annually on weed control.
