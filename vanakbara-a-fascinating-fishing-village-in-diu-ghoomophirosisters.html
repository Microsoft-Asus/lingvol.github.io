At the extreme west of the Diu island, there lies a fascinating little fishing village called Vanakbara. And yes it is one of the highlights of Diu.
It’s great to wander around this village, packed with colorful fishing boats, some all set to sail while some are getting repaired. he village is bustling with activity & best time is to go early morning 7am to 8am when the fishing fleet returns and sells off its catch.
