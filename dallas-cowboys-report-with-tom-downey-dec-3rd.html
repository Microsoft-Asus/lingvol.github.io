Dallas Cowboys rumors, news, and a Cowboys Mock Draft with trades on today’s Cowboys Report! Join host Tom Downey as he takes you through everything going on around the Cowboys as they get ready to play the Ravens. That includes discussion on Ezekiel Elliott, a Sam Darnold trade idea, Andy Dalton and Post Malone’s wild Cowboys chain. This week’s Cowboys mock draft has trades. Of course, there are also 2 Live Cowboys mailbags.

Cowboys Rumors today include notes for Week 13 vs. the Ravens and a look ahead to the Cowboys offseason. Is it finally time to play Reggie Robinson and Cedrick Wilson more? And will Andy Dalton be back next year and/or should the Cowboys go trade for Sam Darnold? Is Hunter Niswander the Cowboys punter of the present and future? And what is going on with Post Malone’s chain?

Dallas Cowboys draft focuses on our first NFL Mock Draft with trades of the year for the Cowboys. It goes all 7-rounds for the 2021 NFL Draft as host Tom Downey attempts to fix the Cowboys. Much can and will change, but based on the current Cowboys Rumors and current NFL Draft order, these mock draft picks could help the Cowboys in a big way.

Cowboys trade rumors will heat up in the offseason. But trading for Sam Darnold to replace Dak Prescott? Cowboys Report host Tom Downey isn’t a big fan of that idea. Although, Andy Dalton might not be back with the Cowboys in 2021, so they might have to find a new backup QB.

The Cowboys are struggling in 2020, that’s just the reality of this season. So why not play some young guys in Reggie Robinson and Cedrick Wilson? The Cowboys finally tried out a new punter in Hunter Niswander, who had never really played in the NFL. And guess what - he’s been actually really good!

Cowboys draft buzz will start to heat up as the Cowboys trend towards a top 5 pick. Could they get OT Penei Sewell? And if so, where do they play him?

Post Malone is a known Cowboys fan. And his latest chain is out there. And well, you’ll just have to see how much it costs.
