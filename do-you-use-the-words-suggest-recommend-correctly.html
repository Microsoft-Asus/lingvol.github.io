Do you USE the words SUGGEST & RECOMMEND correctly?
I SUGGEST you WATCH this video to find out.
I RECOMMEND IMPLEMENTING my advice..

When you use 'recommend' with a VERB you should say,
" I recommend YOU READ this book”.
However, in the PASSIVE form you can say,
“Someone is recommended TO DO something = Students are recommended TO READ the following books.”
