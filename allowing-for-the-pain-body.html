Allowing for the Pain Body
Allowing Yourself to Experience Discomfort
• this is the widest doorway to remaining present to the pain body and allowing it to discharge, dissolve.
A handy tip for becoming more free of pain-body that is often associated with early trauma.
• Pause, relax open to notice discomfort and the energy that wants to fight, jump, engage with negative thought patterns.
• Decide not to hook in and identify with this energy. Let yourself feel discomfort.
• Choose not to be a host for it to feed on
• Become comfortable with the toxic field that may be activated so it can move through you, transmute and empty.
• Continue to widen and notice a natural wellspring of restorative calm and quiet that implodes the emotional body field when you do not feed the pain body argument.
• Root and anchor in the still, calm openness of your true self.
