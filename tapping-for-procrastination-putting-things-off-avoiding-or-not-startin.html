Pam leads you through an easy to follow tapping routine to release the resistance to starting something new.

Do you think you need to be an expert before you even start or try something new?

Maybe you have thoughts like:

I don't want to make mistakes or stuff it up
I don't know enough
I don't have the information
I am not qualified
I'm going to get it all wrong
What if I fail?
I don't want to look like an idiot
I need to get this right...straight off the bat

Take the time to address all the ways this resonates with you and what stops you from doing what it is you want to do.
