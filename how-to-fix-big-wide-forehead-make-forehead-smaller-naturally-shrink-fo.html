1 minute massage. Start from up and down.
Left to right massage.
Circular massage.

Step 2
Massage the area in circular motions and slowly move it up and down. Do this for 1 minute.
Massage along the temples will help stimulate blood circulation.

Step 3
Use your fingers to place your scalp and forehead.
