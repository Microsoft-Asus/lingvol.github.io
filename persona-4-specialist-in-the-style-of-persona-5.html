The music of the Persona series is some of my favorite stuff ever, so I thought it would be cool to reimagine a Persona 4 track as if it was a part of Persona 5's OST - Enjoy!

I produced this arrangement based on both the version of the song in the original P4 OST and the remixed version in the "Reincarnation" album, using "Life Goes On" from P5 as a base for the sound I was trying to achieve. Persona 3 fans may notice a familiar phrase somewhere in here too ;)
