DOLL OBSESSED 17-year-old, Felipe Adams, is hoping to follow in the footsteps of Roddy Alves to become the latest human Ken doll on the block. Felipe, who is currently too young to get plastic surgery, wakes up at 2:30am every day so he can arrive at school for 7am fully transformed as a human Ken doll. Felipe told Truly: “It takes exactly four hours to put on my clothes, shoes, hair, to do the whole makeup, every detail, everything takes exactly four hours." But since he is too young and cannot afford surgery, from the age of 15 years old, Felipe has been teaching himself mind-blowing makeup skills that allow him to look like the world-famous doll. His talent has fooled others into believing he has undergone multiple rounds of surgery, yet Felipe only dreams of fulfilling this life long dream once he is an adult and able to afford it.
