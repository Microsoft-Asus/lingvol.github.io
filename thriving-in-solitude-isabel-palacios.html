My loves... Today's video is on how to deal with loneliness, even diminish it, and actually thrive in SOLITUDE! Solitude is magical, rejuvenating, peaceful, luxurious, and joyful! I used to have major FOMO, and by forcing myself to do things on my own, recharging away from people, and using solitude to get ahead on my goals, I am truly happiest when alone. It might be a Pisces or empath thing, but I truly truly enjoy it! When you enjoy solitude, your self esteem goes up, you are more confident in your skin, and you protect your energy! Hope these tips on how to enjoy your own company and help you diminish your feelings of loneliness or company 24/7. Remember, it's always important to be your own best friend first. Love you guys!
