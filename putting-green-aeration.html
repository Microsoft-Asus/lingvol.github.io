This is a quick little sneak peek into the turfgrass management process we use on our greens here at Schneiter's Golf. Aeration is the process of creating holes in the surface of the turfgrass soil layer to allow enhanced air and moisture flow. Roots also grow deeper and fuller with less ground to work against. Its a great benefit to the turfgrass and the putting surface.
