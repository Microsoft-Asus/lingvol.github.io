In this lesson, I have explained the word "Constantly" with examples. Learn this English vocabulary word and use it in your daily conversation. It will help in improving your English Fluency.

Meaning:
All the time, always
Sentences:
1. My business is constantly growing.
2. He is constantly changing his plans for the future.
3. My boss is constantly keeping me on my toes.
