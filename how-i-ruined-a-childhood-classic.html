Recently I had the opportunity to participate in a really cool project where a group of YouTube composers, including Aimee Nolte, David Bruce, 8-Bit Music Theory, and Adam Neely, all took famous children's songs and stuffed them full of extreme rhythms, and since Adam only gave us like a minute to explain ourselves in the group video, I thought it'd be fun to break down all the things I did to turn Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star from a simple kid's song into a rhythmic nightmare.
