What is renters insurance and how much do I need? That's the topic of today's video. Renters insurance is coverage purchased by the renter of a house, apartment, condo, etc. It's meant to protect the renter in case of damage that is the fault of the renter. Oftentimes, the homeowners insurance policy held by the property owner does not cover the personal effects of the renter. Renters insurance will also often cover theft. Essentially, if you're renting a place you will want to seriously consider getting renters insurance.
