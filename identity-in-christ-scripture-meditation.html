// MORE ABOUT MY SCRIPTURE MEDITATIONS
I've never dealt with more insecurity than this past year and the only thing that honestly helps is reading or saying God's truth or listening to scripture read aloud. I have a friend who started recording herself reading bible scriptures and her prayers and that inspired me to create these guided christian meditation where I read aloud my favorite scriptures on different topics over peaceful music. I commonly use this recording in my morning meditation, as I drive to work, or when I'm trying to go to sleep. It also helps me memorize the scriptures over time just by listening to them over and over! I pray this helps you as well!
