Is video mein Indeed.com ka detail mein demo milega job search aur apply karne ke liye. Indeed ka website aur app dono ka pura usage guide hai is video mein. You can find jobs in India on Indeed.co.in and this video will explain the process in Hindi.

Indeed is a international job site which operates in 60 countries. Indeed is available in India and I believe their product is good. They have a website and a mobile app that works on both Android and iPhone. Searching job on Indeed is easy and the interface is clean. Indeed also has a simple resume building tool using which you can create your CV online on Indeed. It is very helpful and you will see a full demo of that.
