Learn Ragas is a musical insight into certain important and typical ragas of Carnatic Classical Musis. Charulatha Mani, popular Carnatic Vocalist is acknowledged world over as an expert on Ragas. Students and music lovers will love this offering for its simplicity, and user-friendly presentation. This video is completely in lucid English which makes it appealing to a wide spectrum of music loves.
