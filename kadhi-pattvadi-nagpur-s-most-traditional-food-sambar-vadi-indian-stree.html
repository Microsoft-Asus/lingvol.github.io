Patodi is Most Famous Traditional Street Food of Nagpur, Maharastra. It is also knows as Sambar Vadi, Puda chi Vadi or as Kothimbir Vadi. This dish is still staple breakfast for many. Patodi or Patwadi is made from rolled gram flour dough stuffed with coriander, coconut and dry fruits' mixture and deep fried. It is served with kadhi which is made from buttermilk.



Address : Patodi Bhandar, Badkas Chowk, Mahal, Tilak Rd, Nagpur, Maharastra
