MAX – “Acid Dreams” (Studio Performance)

Once you've got the knack for cranking out earworms, you can really turn some heads around. Max Schneider has that knack, and ever since "Lights Down Low" trickled out into the world in 2016 under the single moniker MAX, he's been on a roll. If you'd been paying close attention, you probably know that Schneider had a couple of earlier releases back at the start of the decade. There he revealed his bedrock skills, but these days, with 'Hell's Kitchen Angel' behind him and an endless string of collabs that stretches from Hoody Allen to Noah Cyrus to Illenium to gnash, he's a true pop pro firing on all cylinders. He came into roll through "Acid Dreams" and version of "Love Me Less" that features Quinn XCII. Both are pretty addictive.
