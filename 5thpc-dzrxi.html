Long term investing is obviously a key component for successfully building wealth.

But that does not mean that advisors and clients need to be complacent. A little too much "buy and hold, and we'll get through this" and a little more "here is exactly how we are going to take advantage of the current environment" is required.

If you do not know exactly how your portfolio will adapt when markets fluctuate then you are most often leaving it to chance.

In the end, doing things differently is the only way you can create a different result and therefore a "remarkable" outcome with your portfolio.
