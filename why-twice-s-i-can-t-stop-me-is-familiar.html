Today, we are going to be reviewing Twice’s comeback with I can’t stop me, and talk about my thoughts, why it’s familiar, why it’s not familiar and honestly everything in between. Jokes will be made, but I will make sure to clearly point them out.

Ok, so this music video starts out with this beautiful scene and it features this seemingly floating platform with a beautiful platform that reflects the stars and this flower in the middle. It then opens up and twice is inside. Now I don’t know if you remember my last review, but I expressed how I wish we could have explored this fake type of island they were on in more and more and I think we got to do that here. And so, we shall venture into why it’s familiar. To me this is like a continuation of more and more, you know that song gave us a glimpse into this world, and now we get to dive even more further into it. I mean we see this cliff, these colorful types of mountains that they are dancing on. We just get to see a universe that doesn’t exist but that they created. And then it ends with their train almost going off a cliff, so maybe we’ll get a more and more part 3 within this universe.
