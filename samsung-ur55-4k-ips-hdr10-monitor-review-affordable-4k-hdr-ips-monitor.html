The Samsung UR55 Monitor is an affordable editing and gaming monitor that has a beautiful 28" 4K IPS display with a nearly bezeless design on the side and top of the monitor. The monitor supports HDR10 and has a 10bit format. The colors are very vivid and the picture quality is very detailed.

Samsung 4K UR55 IPS Monitor: https://amzn.to/3ke9vtq
Monitor Arm: https://amzn.to/37tUL62

With the release of the PS5 getting closer, this monitor will be a great addition to that set up for 4K gaming. If you are getting the PS5 Pro then this is definitely a monitor to consider since the ps5 pro caps at 60Hz.

The Samsung UR55 Monitor is a well balanced 4k monitor for content creators and gamers, this time favoring content creators a little more.

The display looks amazing. The colors are very vivid and the clarity is incredible.

The Samsung 4K IPS monitor has a 4ms response time and supports ADM Freesync technology that will match the variable frame rate of your graphics card with that of the monitor to prevent screen tearing or lag. It is something you should definitely take advantage of if you have an AMD graphics card.

The monitor has a gaming mode that enhances the picture quality that will help you distinguish characters from the background. It also offers the picture in picture that is good if you plan on having to different sources plug into this monitor like your pc and gaming console.
