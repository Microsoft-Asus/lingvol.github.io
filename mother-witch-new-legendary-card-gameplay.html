Today on TV Royale it was announced there is a NEW Legendary card, 4 elixir cost Mother Witch. Mother Witch will be the last card added in 2020 in Clash Royale. Today we'll feature some Mother Witch gameplay with SlashWithShane. New balance changes are also coming. Read below:


X-BOW REWORK
Lifetime Reduced 40s to 30s
Hit Speed Slower 0.25 to 0.3s
Damage Increased 30%
Health Reduced 4%

X-Bow has been one of the most polarizing cards ever. We wanted to rework it to keep it a deadly threat but lessen its power as a defensive building. Now the bolts are more powerful than ever, able to kill enemies with fewer shots! But the X-Bow is more fragile, and does not last on the battlefield for nearly as long.

GUARDS
Hitspeed Increased 10%
Health Reduced 32%

This minor rework brings Guards down to the same health and attack speed as Skeletons for consistency. This results in a significant DPS buff for the little guys with a health tradeoff that will often not impact common interactions.
