This is a video from my recent 24-hour stopover flight with Qatar Airways in Doha, Qatar.

You can spend your time at Hamad International airport or you could extend your flight and turn your trip into a layover.

Qatar has a tourist visa-free entry for over 80 countries.

Qatar Airways has many flights connecting Doha with the world.

If you wish to stopover you can book an affordable hotel in advance on the Discover Qatar website with your flight booking.

Break up your flight, explore Doha for 24 hours, get some rest and enjoy a nights stay then fly to your final onwards destination.

Why not explore Doha, Qatar, break up your journey and enjoy an extra destination.

- Qatar National Museum
