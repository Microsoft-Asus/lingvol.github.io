Among other issues, cell phone addiction and excessive hours of computer usage can create a structural problem in the neck and lead to cervical instability and vagus nerve degeneration. In our clinics, patients with chronic neck pain and headaches typically present with many other symptoms which Ross Hauser, MD discusses in this video. One thing that these massively increasing number of cases have in common is the countless amount of hours spent on the computer or looking down at a phone which have exacerbated their structural issue, often to a point that their condition has become completely debilitating.
